Most Popular Dog Breeds of 2016

Rank in 2016 Dog Breed Name Summary Other
1 Labrador Retriever

This dog is gentle, intelligent, and family-friendly. It is the most popular breed in the US. This breed comes in 3 colors, including yellow, black, and brown.
  1. Personality: Friendly and outgoing, play well with others
  2. Energy Level: Very active, high-spirited and not afraid to show it
  3. Life Expectancy: 10-12 years
2 German Shepherd

German Shepherd Dogs are large, agile, and muscular, and very intelligent..
  1. Personality: Smart, confident, courageous, and steady; a true dog lover's dog
  2. Energy Level: Very active, born to work, require regular mental and physical exercise
  3. Life Expectancy: 7-10 years
3 Golden Retriever

This is an exuberant, friendly, strong, and hard working dog. They are good at whatever they do, whether it is hunting, serving as guide dogs, or being devoted companions, but can also be silly.
  1. Personality: Intelligent, friendly, devoted
  2. Energy Level: Very active, active and energetic, needs daily exercise
  3. Life Expectancy: 10-12 years
4 Bulldog

Known for their loose-jointed, shuffling gait and massive, short-faced head, the Bulldog is known to be equable, resolute, and dignified. The Bulldog may be brindle, white, red, fawn, fallow, or piebald.
  1. Personality: Calm, courageous, friendly, dignified by amusing
  2. Energy Level: Not very active, require regular walks and occasional romp
  3. Life Expectancy: 8-10 years
5 Beagle

Not only are Beagles excellent hunting dogs and loyal companions, they are also happy-go-lucky, funny, and cute. They were bred to hunt in packs, so they like the company of other dogs and people. They love to follow their noses. They are solid, sturdy, and fairly easy to care for.
  1. Personality: Merry, friendly, and curious
  2. Energy Level: Very active, high-spirited and not afraid to show it
  3. Life Expectancy: 10-12 years
